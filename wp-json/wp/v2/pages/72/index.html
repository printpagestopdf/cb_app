App for CommonsBooking<\/a> API<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

This app makes it possible to use functions of a website that is operated with the CommonsBooking plugin.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

CommonsBooking websites offer the free sharing of goods (probably in most cases cargo bikes).<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The app displays the offers on a map or in list form.
More detailed information on the items and rental stations is displayed and the availability of the respective items is shown in a calendar.
If you are registered with the platform, you can also make and manage bookings.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

For this to be possible, the website must meet the following requirements:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  • The platform is operated with the CommonsBooking plugin<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • For the display of offers<\/strong>:
    the CommonsAPI (part of CommonsBooking) is activated and freely accessible<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • For booking and managing bookings<\/strong>:
    A login to the website and the activated Extension Plugin „API Extension to support CB App“. The Plugin can be installed from the     WordPress Directory<\/a>.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n
